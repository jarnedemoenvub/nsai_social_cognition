RELATED AI METHODS / MODELS
===============================
- Markov Logic Networks (Richardson & Domingos, 2006): probabilistic logic framework.
- Hybrid MLNs (Wang & Domingos, 2008): extensions of MLNs with continuous features.
- Bayesian Logic (BLOG, Milch et al., 2005): open-domain probabilistic logic.
- Socher et al. (2013): Neural Tensor Networks for knowledge base completion.
- Rocktäschel et al. (2015): injecting logical knowledge into embeddings.
- Semantic/Logic-as-Loss (Xu et al. 2018): encode FOL constraints as differentiable losses.
- Diligenti et al. (2012): bridging logic and kernel machines.
- Bottou (2011): From machine learning to machine reasoning.
- Valiant (1999): Robust Logics.
- d’Avila Garcez, Lamb, Gabbay (2009): Neural-Symbolic Cognitive Reasoning.
